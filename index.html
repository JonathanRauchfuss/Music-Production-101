Music Production-Course Features

During this course you will learn:

How to create, record, edit and produce your own original music using a digital audio wokstation. Each live-instruction session is approxmately 45 minutes. This course is available for all New York City schools. No prior production or music knowledge is needed to take this course. By the end of this course you will create your own original song. You will also be armed with the core knowledge needed for audio production industry jobs. The only requirements needed for the course are a computer running a Mac Operating system and a pair of headphones.
